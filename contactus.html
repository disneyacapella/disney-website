Auditions Inquiries

Any current or incoming UPenn students, graduate or undergraduate, who are interested in auditioning for Disney A Cappella, are welcome to participate in Disney's audition cycles. Disney A Cappella holds auditions typically once a semester within the first two weeks or so of the semester.

Follow our Instagram page to keep up to date on exact details or fill out our auditions interest form here to receive reminders when audition cycles start!
