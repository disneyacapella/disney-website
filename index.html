About Us

We are Penn’s co-ed, community service interest, all-Disney-all-the-time a cappella group. Yes, we only sing Disney songs, by which we mean any song featured on a soundtrack associated with a Disney movie, T.V. show, or Broadway musical. Will we ever run out? Well, it’s time to see what we can do, to test the limits and break through. ​

Since our founding in 2011, we have made it our mission to spread happiness and Disney magic to the community. In addition to our semesterly shows, the proceeds of which we donate to a different charity each performance, we regularly perform in neighborhood hospitals and primary schools, as well as at various events and fundraisers on and off campus.
