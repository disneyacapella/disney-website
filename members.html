Yoora Do

Class of '24  |  Kiara, The Lion King II

Yoora Do is a senior in the College studying political science and perpetually wondering if she should also major in history. She was born in Seoul, South Korea, but she has also spent some time in California and Virginia. In her free time, she likes to watch movies, play the guitar, and annoy her family by belting Disney songs, Korean ballads, and Broadway tunes. 

Sinead Knepper

Class of '24  |  Cinderella, Cinderella

Sinead is a senior in the college studying East Asian Languages and Civilizations and minoring in Anthropology, with interests in international management and business. She loves singing (obviously) and acting, and you can usually find her rehearsing for a show, looking for great food places, or doing homework while blasting "Disney Villain's Greatest Hits". She is so excited to live out her lifelong Disney princess dreams by being part of Disney A Cappella with such an amazing group of people.  

Charlie Schumer

Class of '24  |  Woody, Toy Story

Charlie is a senior in the College studying political science and economics. He hails from Minneapolis, MN so he cooks up a mean tater tot hot dish and wears shorts in February. Outside of Disney, Charlie is involved in Student Government and research. A fun fact is that Charlie is legally an honorary Pennsylvanian. What does that mean? Ask him to find out! Charlie has loved his time so far in Disney, and can't wait for what's to come!

Harrison Beckler

Class of '25  |  Han Solo, Star Wars

Harrison is a junior in the College from New York majoring in Cinema and Media Studies. Harrison aspires to make his top-secret Star Wars script a reality. He’s heard that Disney shreds, burns, or defenestrates unsolicited scripts, but never tell him the odds.

Jill Rayca

Class of '25  |  Jack Sparrow, Pirates of the Carribean

Jill is a Philly native studying Digital Media Design with a minor in Mechanical Engineering. When not rehearsing for Disney A Cappella with their fellow altos, Jill loves to draw, annoy their roommates by belting out showtunes while studying, and compose classic rock songs on their keyboard or ukulele (Queen is a personal favorite)! They are always down for a Marvel/Star Wars movie marathon and/or deep discussion about it, and are so excited to be a part of Disney! 

Mia Kim

Class of '25 |  Bambi, Bambi

Mia is a junior studying Cell and Molecular Biology in the college. She is from California and loves warm weather, but is excited for the snow in Pennsylvania! Mia loves spending time with her cousins, crocheting, and blasting karaoke, and her hope for the future is to help people and spread smiles by researching disease mechanisms and interventions. Mia is so excited to be a part of Disney A Cappella family!

Jinny Chung

Class of '26  |  Anna, Frozen

Jinny is a sophomore from Seoul, South Korea (where her cat Nermal resides). She is planning to major in Physics and Astronomy in the college. Despite being a STEM kid, she has always been passionate about the arts, whether that is singing, dancing, drawing or playing the piano. At Penn, she is also involved in various research journals/clubs and the Daily Pennsylvanian. In her free time, Jinny likes to watch anime, collect memes, and play games. Her all-time favorite Disney movies are Tangled, Frozen, and Mulan. She is so happy to be a part of Disney A Cappella!

Donna Jalosjos

Class of '26  |  Raya, Raya and the Last Dragon

Donna is a sophomore from Topeka, Kansas, studying at the Wharton School of Business. She joined Disney A Cappella as a freshman and is super excited to continue her a cappella adventure throughout college. In her free time, she likes to cook, learn languages for fun, karaoke, and drink iced chai lattes. If she's not in rehearsal, you can probably find her visiting Chinatown for the 100th time. Outside of Disney A Cappella, she is also heavily involved in the Penn Glee Club. Her favorite thing about Disney is how close the members are to the alumni!
